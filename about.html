How it works

  1. Make a booking

    • You will need to type out a designated location of which our self-driving car can locate and pick you up. This is called the origin point, also called, point 'A', on the map.
    • Next, choose a destination, a location where our A.I. automated cars can take you. This is marked on the map as point 'B'. Please bear in mind. It will only deliver you from a location designated in the same country/region.
    • Our map will then create the most efficient route for the car to travel along, from point 'A' to point 'B'.
    • You can then set a time and date for your journey. You will see a quick summary of your booking before you continue to personalizing your experience.
